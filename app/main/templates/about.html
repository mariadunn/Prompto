Prompto!

...what is it?

Prompto is a character development tool.

Create your worlds, Populate them with characters and then use the prompt tools in Write to throw them into new and unusual situations.

Prompto is an organisation tool.

Ever written an awesome one-shot about your favourite ship, only to have it disappear into the maze of Google Docs (or your word-processing software of choice)? With the filter tools in Stories - or the character pages in Worlds - you can easily find which stories your characters feature in. You can also add (or remove) characters at any time as your story develops.

Prompto wants you to write!

With minimal set-up, you can get inspired and get writing in just a few clicks! Prompto may not be suitable for developing your next novel (though you're welcome to try), but perfect for any short stories, drabbles or one-shots you feel compelled to pen. Stories can be edited and downloaded once they are created - just find the story in Stories to see your options.

...who is it for?

Prompto is for anyone who finds it useful, but was built with character-driven writers in mind, as well as any author that finds themselves with a limited amount of time to actually dedicate to their craft.

...who made it?

Prompto was created by me, Maria Dunn. Hello! I am a novice developer and mother-of-two who has been scribbling stories since I was old enough to hold a pencil. Having very little time to dedicate to this passion now, I wanted to create an app that would help people in similar situations to get writing, even if it's just for a few minutes a day.

Prompto was created with Python and JavaScript, using the Flask framework. As a solo project by an amateur programmer, please be aware that bugs may happen - don't rely on Prompto as your primary method of storing your writing. Thank you for using Prompto!
